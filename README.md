## Coleman Matey

This is a project designed to showcase my professional background, skills, and selected projects. The site is intended to be an online resume and contact point for potential employers, collaborators, and clients.

---

## ✨ Planned Sections

- **Home** – Introduction and personal branding
- **About Me** – Background, education, and skills overview
- **Projects** – Highlight of key projects with descriptions and visuals
- **Resume** – Downloadable and interactive version of my resume
- **Contact** – Form and social links for direct communication
